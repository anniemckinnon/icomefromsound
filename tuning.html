SITE TUNING

I’ve been wondering how might it dawn on someone that their own actions might be contributing to the soundscape around them? At an individual level, are we listening, or are we tuned out? If we are tuned out – how do we tune in? A sound engineer might try to make something sound in tune, or sound better by making the musicians tune their instruments, with a tuner. Professional musicians, those you might see in a chamber orchestra, only need to hear one another to tune. Before we are to become better at listening to a site and to each other – we need to have a greater sense of the “frequencies” – we are listening for. We need a site tuner. This is my first attempt at developing one.
